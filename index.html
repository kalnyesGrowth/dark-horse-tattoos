Our Story

The Dark Horse Story

Since 2008, Dark Horse Tattoos has been Stafford, Virginia's home for custom ink. What started as a passion for permanent art has grown into one of the region's most trusted studios, with five talented artists who bring over fifty years of combined experience to every session.

Every piece we create is a collaboration between artist and client. We work exclusively by appointment to give each person our undivided attention in a clean, private, and comfortable environment. No rush, no shortcuts, just the kind of work that earns trust for life.

Before Your Session

How to Prepare

Do

Arrive on time for your appointment
Get adequate sleep the night before
Stay hydrated and eat a solid meal
Wear loose, comfortable clothing
Bring reference images and music

Don't

Drink alcohol the night before or day of
Take painkillers (they thin the blood)
Apply numbing cream (prohibited by law)
Bring more than one guest or children
Use recreational drugs before your session
